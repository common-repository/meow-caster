Meow Caster is here for make it easy to add embed Youtube video and playlist, make Youtube video galleries in WordPress.

== Description ==

Need to embed a video, playlist or gallery of video ?

That's the primary objective of Meow Caster and make it easy.

It's a first step before more feature in order to bring all you need for YouTube in your WordPress.

Need a little more like... import playlists as galleries ? access your private content and unlisted ?
We work on a [Premium Version](https://meow.codes/meow-caster/ "Meow Caster Premium") to go further.
